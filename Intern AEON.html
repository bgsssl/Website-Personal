Main Responsibilities:

  • Support daily fresh food operations in accordance with AEON SOPs for the fish & seafood category, including inspection of receipts, display, and daily quality control
  • Conducting inspections of incoming goods, grading, and quality control of fresh seafood products.
  • Implementing FIFO/FEFO stock management to reduce the risk of quality deterioration and waste.
  • Assisting with display arrangement, merchandising, and customer-oriented product presentation.
  • Coordinate with supervisors and store teams to ensure operational efficiency.

Impact

  • Ensured smooth daily fresh food operations for the fish & seafood category by consistently implementing AEON SOPs, including receiving inspections, product display management, and routine quality control to maintain product freshness and food safety standards.
  • Performed incoming goods inspection, seafood grading, and quality control processes to ensure only products meeting company quality standards were accepted and displayed for sale.
  • Applied FIFO/FEFO inventory management practices to optimize stock rotation, minimize product deterioration, reduce waste, and improve inventory efficiency.
  • Supported merchandising activities and customer-oriented product presentation by arranging attractive, hygienic, and well-organized seafood displays to enhance the shopping experience and increase product visibility.
  • Collaborated closely with supervisors and cross-functional store teams to maintain operational efficiency, support daily store activities, and ensure smooth execution of fresh food operations.
